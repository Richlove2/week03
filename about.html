Whitewater Rafting

History

Founded in 1985 by river enthusiast John Doe, Whitewater Rafting began as a small operation with just two rafts. Today, we're the premier rafting company in the region, offering unforgettable adventures on some of the most exciting rivers.

Our mission is to provide safe, exciting, and memorable rafting experiences while preserving the natural beauty of our rivers. We're proud to have introduced over 50,000 adventurers to the thrill of whitewater rafting.
